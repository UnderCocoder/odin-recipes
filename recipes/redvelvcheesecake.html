Red Velvet Cheesecake

So you want to create a delicious, moist, and fluffy Red Velvet Cheesecake? You have come to the right place my friend!

Below I will list the necessary preparation and steps to create your very own Red Velvet Cheesecake! All at the comfort of your own kitchen, all of the ingredients listed will be common household names; we all know how much we hate going to the store for one ingredient!

Ingredients

To get started, you will first need the following:

  • 2 1/2 cups self-rising flour
  • 1 1/2 cups white sugar
  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon distilled white vinegar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 2 ounces red food coloring
  • 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cup butter, softened
  • 4 cups confectioners' sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup chopped pecans

Steps

Make sure to properly follow the steps below, failure to do so will result in TOTAL ANNIHILATION!!!!

  1. Preheating the oven
    2. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour three 8 inch round pans.
  2. Mixing ingredients
    3. In a large bowl, mix together sugar, oil, and eggs. Add food coloring and vinegar to buttermilk. Add baking soda to flour. Add flour mixture and buttermilk mixtures alternately to the sugar mixture. Mix well. Stir 1 teaspoon vanilla into batter. Pour batter into prepared pans.
  3. Baking
    4. Bake for 20 to 25 minutes, or until done. Remove from oven, and cool on wire racks.
  4. Last Mix
    5. Mix together cream cheese, butter or margarine, confectioners' sugar, 1 teaspoon vanilla. Stir in nuts. Frost cooled cake.
