Hot Banana Water

This was my grandmother's recipe. She made us drink it. Many fond memories. Serve warm in mugs with whipped cream and marshmallows for an extra treat.

Ingredients

Here are the ingredients for you to make your very own Hot Banana Water!

  • 1 quart water
  • 2 banana peels

Steps

Make sure to follow these directions thoroughly for the best experience!

  1. Cut off the ends of the banana peels. Bring water to a boli in a large pot. Add 1 banana peel to boliing water.
  2. Turn off heat, cover, and wait for 10 minutes.
  3. Add 1 more banana peel
  4. Wait 10 more minutes, stir to incorporate flavors
  5. Strain into mugs and serve warm
